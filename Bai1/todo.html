6amMart - Multivendor Food, Grocery, eCommerce, Parcel, Pharmacy delivery app with Admin & Website


6amMart is a Larvarel and Flutter Framwork-based multi-vendor food, grocety, eCommerce, parcel, and pharmacy delivery system. It has six modules to cover all your business functionality.

TOP FEATURES OF ADMIN & STORE PANEL


All on one Powerful Complete Multivendor Online Food Delivery Script

Accurate Business Zone Map

You can messsage numerous zones for your business converage area in the admin panel. The map drawing tool can assist you in precisely drawing your converage. These zones can have stones added to them. Items can only be ordered by users who are covered in that location.

Multiple Stores

6amMart - food delivery app can overate multiple stores according to your needs. As a result, you can create an endless number of stores and easily manage them from the admin panel.

OTP integration through SMS

This Food delivery system is developed with all modern technologies. OTP is significant among them for security and spam protection. You can easily integrate and verify your user.


Management of Items

You may manage categories and subcategories, attributes, add-ons, and move in item area. Any store can have items added by themselves. A function for managing items will also be added to the Store panel.

Advanced Order Management

This Multivendor delivery system has an advanced order management process for both admin and store manager and delivery man. You can have live experience by using our demo.

Management of Dispatch

Dispatch Management can assist you in swiftly determing which orders are active and which order are awaiting delivery. These features will definitely help you to accelerate your business process.


Delivery Men Apps

You may establish an endless number of delivery men, who can quickly accept orders, delivery them, and earn money using the delivery men app. It is a great opportunity to earn money through this app. From the admin panel, you may choose between a freelance delivery man and a salaried delivery man.

Manual assign of delivery man

The 6amMart admin panel will provide you with yet another useful feature, you can assign an available delivery man immediately.

Marketing Section

This online food delivery app script will offer you a feature-rich admin panel from with you can manage all of the primary marketing aspects. Campaigns, ads, coupons, and push notifications are just a few of the features that will help you build your business quickly.


Section of Accounts

You can see the store and delivery men's earnings, handle withdrawals, physically collect amounts of cash, and manage a large number of employees. Nothing is impossible in this PHP Lavarel-based delivery app script.

Statistics and Reporting

Full of Statistics and reports, you'll get a visual report of your company's cash flow and financial status. This will be shown as day and item-wise reports.

Employee Management

If you are facing any problem in employee managements, you can solve this issue by using the employee section. You can manage the role of employees and make a list of your new employees.

Complete Multi-purpose Grocery Delivery Script


We develop this script for the multipurpose delivery system. You can use it as food delivery app or use it as a grocery, parcel delivery app, no matter what your business type is. You will get all the above-mentioned features along with these unique business features.


Business Set-up

Business Settings can assist you in establishing your company in the manner that you desire. You'll have a lot of options for customizing your business.

Complete Featured User App

This bundle includes the source code for the 6amMart mobile app. We used Flutter to create this app, which can be used on both Android and iOS.

Multiple Payment Gateways

6amMart has several payment gateways built in. All of them are well-known, and you may get started right way. If you can't find your desired payment gateway, you contact us for your customized payment gateway.

Both iOS and Android Supported Food Delivery App Script(Flutter App).


6amMart mobile app is developed through extensive UI/UX design. So the app is very user-friendly and your user will be satisfied after they install the app. Go through our demo.
